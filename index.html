About Me


Get to know me!

"Meet Cooky! She’s a lovable mongrel with a heart full of affection and a personality as unique as her mix. Cooky is the ultimate cuddle buddy—clingy at times, but in the sweetest way. She’s a true lovable dog, getting extra excited whenever someone arrives, always ready to shower them with love and attention. Though she’s as smart as they come, she definitely knows how to play the independent card when she feels like it."

"However, don’t let her independent streak fool you; Cooky absolutely hates being alone! She’ll stick to you like glue, especially when it’s time for a nap. Speaking of naps, she sleeps a lot—her dream life includes lounging around and getting all the cuddles. But when it comes to meals, she’s definitely got an appetite and eats like she’s preparing for a marathon!"

A little bit of a diva when it comes to bath time, though—let's just say, it’s not her favorite activity. But despite her quirks, she’s irresistibly cute and always ready to melt your heart with those big, loving eyes.

Cooky’s the type of dog who makes sure you never feel alone, and her love is constant, unconditional, and always heartwarming.

Cooky's Personalities

Snack Snatcher

Cooky's always on high alert for food. If there’s a snack in the room, you can bet she’ll find a way to sneak it, whether it’s on the table or in your hand. Blink, and it’s gone!

Couch Potato

She’s a champion of relaxation. Give her a comfy spot, and Cooky will transform into a living, breathing pillow. Your lap? She’ll claim it, even if you had other plans for the space.

The Detective

Cooky's nose is always at work. She’s sniffing out every crumb, mystery smell, or new scent like it’s a top-secret investigation. If you dropped a chip under the couch, don’t expect it to last long.

The Guard Dog

Cooky may be a softie at heart, but she’s got an impressive protective streak. Whether it’s the delivery person at the door or a car pulling into the driveway, she’ll alert the entire neighborhood with a bark

The Wiggly Worm

When Cooky's happy, she’s practically a puddle of joy. You’ll see her do this adorable wriggling dance, her entire body wiggling with excitement. Whether she’s about to greet you after a long day or getting ready for a walk.

The Lick Monster

Some dogs lick to show affection—Cooky licks to dominate. If she’s not giving you a full-on face wash, she’s probably licking her paws, your legs, or the air itself.

The Bath-Time Protester

Cooky absolutely hates baths. The moment she hears the water running, her ears go flat, and she turns into a four-legged escape artist. She’ll try to hide under the table, sneak into the sofa, or give you the "Are you kidding me?" look

The Cuddle Monster

Don’t expect her to respect personal space. If there’s a bed or a blanket, Bella will worm her way in, pinning you down with love (and a little bit of drool). If you stop petting her? Prepare for the softest, most persistent paw on your leg.
